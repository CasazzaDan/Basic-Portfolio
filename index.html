About Me

My name is Dan Casazza. I was born in New Jersey, and moved to Minnesota in 2012. It has been a interesting 7 years to say the least. My main focus when I moved here was to find a better career-path/lifestyle, which led me to starting my own painting LLC in 2016. Although the freedom and self-accomplishment of running a business is hard to be matched, I decided it was best to find a more steady career option. Coding has been a hobby of mine for the past few years, and the when the option to work for a SalesForce Consulting company came along I knew I had to dedicate a lot more time into practicing and improving my coding knowledge.
